# Company Employee Handbook

## Vacation Policy
All full-time employees are entitled to 15 days of paid vacation per calendar year. 
New employees can start using vacation days after completing 3 months of employment. 
Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days.

## Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave annually. 
Sick leave does not carry over to the next year.
